Frequently Asked Questions

Booking a ride is simple! Just enter your pickup location and destination in our app, select your preferred vehicle type, and choose from available drivers. You can track your ride in real-time and pay securely through the app.

We accept various payment methods including mobile money (MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money), credit/debit cards, and cash payments. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

Our fare calculation is based on distance, time, and ride type. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees. You'll see the estimated fare before booking, and the final amount is calculated at the end of your trip.

Safety is our top priority. All drivers are verified and undergo background checks. We provide real-time tracking, emergency contacts, and 24/7 customer support. All vehicles are regularly inspected and insured.

Yes, you can cancel your ride through the app. Free cancellation is available within 2 minutes of booking. After that, a small cancellation fee may apply depending on the timing and circumstances.

To become a driver, you need a valid driver's license, vehicle registration, insurance, and must pass our verification process. Sign up through our driver portal and complete the onboarding process to start earning.
